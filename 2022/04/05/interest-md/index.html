Interest rate

  • required rate of returns is

    • affeted by the supply and demand of funds in the market .
    • minimum rate of return
    • usually for particular investment

  • discount rate is

    • the interest rate we use to discount payment to be made in the future .
    • usually used interchangeably with interest rate

  • Opportunity cost is

    • also understand as a form of interest rate .

Decomposition of interest rate

  • Decompose required rate of return
    • Norminal risk-free rate = real risk-free rate + expected inflation rate

      expected inflation rate 是未知的，是期望的

    • required interest rate on a security = nominal risk-free rate + default risk premium + liquidity premium + maturity risk premium

      risk premium 风险溢价

EAR （effective annual rate 有效年利率）

Holding Period return

  • 做年化 单利 ，本金参与利息,默认360天

  • 复利，compound 利滚利，默认是356天
